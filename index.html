Privacy Policy

Facebook Ads Management via Telegram

Last Updated: February 3, 2026
1

Introduction

Welcome to Moltbot ("we," "our," or "us"). Moltbot is a Telegram bot service that enables you to manage your Facebook advertising campaigns directly through Telegram. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

By using Moltbot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2

Information We Collect

To provide our Facebook Ads management service through Telegram, we collect and process the following information:

  • Telegram Account Information: Your Telegram user ID and username to identify and authenticate you within the bot.
  • Facebook Ads Account Data: When you connect your Facebook Ads account, we access campaign data, ad performance metrics, and account information necessary to display and manage your ads.
  • Commands and Interactions: The commands and messages you send to the bot to fulfill your requests.
  • Access Tokens: OAuth tokens provided by Facebook to authorize our access to your ads account.
3

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity and connect to your Facebook Ads account
  • Display your advertising campaigns, ad sets, and ads within Telegram
  • Execute commands you issue to manage your Facebook Ads (e.g., pause, resume, modify campaigns)
  • Provide performance metrics and insights about your advertising
  • Improve and maintain our service functionality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information or advertising data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4

Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Access tokens are stored securely using encryption
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Access to user data is restricted to essential service operations only
  • We do not store Facebook Ads content or creative assets permanently
5

Facebook Data Usage

Our use of information received from Facebook APIs adheres to the Facebook Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

  • We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our service
  • Facebook data is used exclusively for the purposes disclosed to you
  • We do not use Facebook data for purposes unrelated to Moltbot's functionality
  • You can revoke Moltbot's access to your Facebook account at any time through your Facebook settings
6

Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide our service:

  • Access tokens are stored while your account is connected
  • Interaction logs may be retained for up to 30 days for troubleshooting
  • Upon disconnecting your account, we will delete your stored tokens and associated data within 30 days
7

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data by disconnecting your account
  • Revocation: Revoke Moltbot's access to your Facebook account at any time
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

8

Third-Party Services

Moltbot integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Telegram: The messaging platform through which you interact with Moltbot. Please review Telegram's Privacy Policy.
  • Meta/Facebook: The advertising platform you manage through Moltbot. Please review Meta's Privacy Policy.
9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
